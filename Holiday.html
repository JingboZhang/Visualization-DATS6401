Number of Rides

In general, less people choose to ride bikes during holidays. The percentages of members' rides on Thanksgiving Day and Christmas Eve are similar to that of a regular weekday. However, the percentages of members' rides on Black Friday (the day after Thanksgiving Day) and Christmas Day are similar to a regular weekend.

Duration Distribution

Compared with regular weekdays and regular weekends, the duration distribution of rides in most holidays is similar to the distribution on weekends, except Thanksgiving Day.

Rides Distribution by Hours

For hourly distribution, the shape of distribution on Thanksgiving Day is similar to a regular weekday with a earlier second peak. Rides on Black Friday are more concentrated. The shapes of distribution on Christmas Eve and Christmas Day are similar to a regular weekend but rides are more fluctuated.
